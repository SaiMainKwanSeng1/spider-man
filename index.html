Spider-Man's origin story is a poignant tale of Peter Parker, a high school student from Queens, New York, who is bitten by a radioactive spider during a science experiment. This bite imbues him with incredible spider-like abilities: superhuman strength, agility, and the ability to cling to walls. Initially, Peter uses his newfound powers for personal gain, but a tragic event—the murder of his Uncle Ben due to Peter's inaction—teaches him the invaluable lesson that "with great power comes great responsibility." From that moment, Peter Parker adopts the mantle of Spider-Man, dedicating himself to fighting crime and protecting the innocent.
